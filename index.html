OBMS is a Banking and Finance Management System aims to create a system that is simple to use and improves bank transactions.

About Us

The goal of this project is to fulfill a portion of the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology requirements.
The design and development of Online Banking Transaction Management provide a more secure way of managing bank customer information,
building strong relationships between banks and their customers by providing proper solutions that use multi-level confidentiality to
increase customer satisfaction and provide an easy way of communicating with the bank and the client.
